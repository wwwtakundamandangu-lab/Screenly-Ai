Compliance & Legal Standards

At Screenly AI, we prioritize fairness, transparency, and the security of your data.

Fair Housing Act Statement

Screenly AI is fully committed to the Fair Housing Act. Our AI screening algorithms are strictly programmed to ignore protected characteristics, including race, color, religion, sex, handicap, familial status, or national origin. Every applicant is evaluated solely on financial reliability and rental history.

Data Protection & Privacy

We use bank-level encryption to handle sensitive information, including bank statements and utility bills used in our $20 Inclusive Tier. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

FCRA Compliance

As a consumer reporting agency, we comply with the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA). Applicants have the right to dispute any information found in their screening report and will be notified of any adverse actions taken based on our AI's findings.
